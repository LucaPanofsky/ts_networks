# TEST METHODOLOGY 

For a given module, proceed in this way:

- Understand what the module does and what has been tested

Then, from what we know we need to derive:

**capabilities**
- capabilities are testable staments of the kind 'iCanDoThisFeature'

**constraints**
- constraints are testable statement of the kind 'itMustBethat..'

**negative tests**
- negative tests are very powerful for documenting implicit and hidden assumptions that cant be revealed otherwise

**units**
Sometimes unit tests are just useful and they are tests that are necessary but that cannot be represented otherwise.
